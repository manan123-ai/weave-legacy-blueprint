Our Premium Woven Fabric Range

Janki Nath & Co. manufactures and exports a comprehensive catalogue of premium woven fabrics for luxury fashion, high-street apparel, home furnishing and technical applications. All fabrics are produced in-house across our weaving units in Meerut, Bhiwandi, Ichalkaranji, Erode, Salem and Surat with strict quality control at every stage.

  • Cotton fabrics — plain weave cotton (100–300 GSM), twill cotton (150–350 GSM), sateen cotton (200–400 GSM), poplin, mercerized and sanforized cotton for shirts, dresses, casual wear and bedding.
  • Cotton blends — cotton-polyester (65/35), cotton-linen, cotton-modal and cotton-viscose blends for workwear, activewear and smart casual.
  • Linen fabrics — 100% pure linen, linen blends and linen shirting (120–300 GSM) for summer clothing, home textiles and luxury garments.
  • Jacquard fabrics — damask jacquard, brocade, matelassé, cutwork jacquard and custom designer jacquards (200–400 GSM) for luxury apparel and upholstery.
  • Viscose fabrics — plain viscose, viscose twill and viscose crepe with silk-like drape for evening wear, linings and lightweight garments.
  • Twills & drills — cotton twill, drill fabric, gabardine (180–350 GSM) for workwear, pants and uniforms.
  • Upholstery & home furnishing — curtain fabrics, sofa upholstery, cushion covers and decorative drapery (250–600 GSM, widths up to 140").
  • Crepe & high twist — georgette, moss crepe, high twist voile for formal wear, dresses and blouses.
  • Yarn-dyed fabrics — multi-color, checks and stripes with colorfast properties.
  • Indigo dyes — light to deep indigo shades for denim and traditional wear.
  • Dobby — geometric and abstract dobby weaves for formal shirts and premium textiles.
  • Voiles & cambric — lightweight sheer fabrics (80–140 GSM).
  • Poplin & sheetings — smooth plain weave fabrics for shirts and bedding.
  • Oxfords & canvas — sturdy basket weave fabrics (200–450 GSM).
  • Tweeds — herringbone and houndstooth (300–500 GSM) for coats and suits.
  • Lurex & sequins — metallic and decorative fabrics for evening wear.
  • IKAT & tie dye — traditional resist-dyed patterns.
  • Lycra & lycra blends — 2-way and 4-way stretch fabrics for activewear and swimwear.
  • Custom developed fabrics — fully tailored to client specifications including Greige, RFD, Bleached, Dyed, Printed, Mill-made, Auto/Powerloom and Handloom.

Our Production Process — 6 Steps from Yarn to Finished Fabric

  1. Raw material selection & quality check (1–2 days) — Premium raw materials carefully selected and tested.
  2. Material preparation & processing (2–3 days) — Sorted by specification and processed using advanced techniques.
  3. Loom setup & warping (1 day) — Skilled technicians set up looms with precise tension and alignment.
  4. Weaving process (3–7 days) — Master weavers operate state-of-the-art looms combining tradition with technology.
  5. Quality control & finishing (1–2 days) — Thorough inspection, finishing treatments and final quality checks.
  6. Packaging & dispatch (1 day) — Industry-standard packaging with full export documentation.
